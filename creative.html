CREATIVE PUBLICATION DESIGN

Duration: 12 weeks (108 hours)

Lecture Days: 3 times/week (3 hours/day)

Certificate Awarded: Diploma in Creative Publication Design

Professional Certification: Adobe Certified Associate (Photoshop)

Courses:

  • Adobe InDesign
  • CorelDraw X7
  • Adobe Photoshop

Overall Objectives:

At the end of the programme, participants would be able to:

  • Effectively use Adobe InDesign
  • Effectively use CorelDraw
  • Effectively use Adobe Photoshop

Adobe InDesign Course Outline:

  • Workspace basics
  • Layouts
  • Frames and objects
  • Drawing
  • The basics of working with text
  • Styles
  • Combining text with objects
  • Setting up tables
  • Colours
  • The basics of working with text
  • EPUB for InDesign CS5.5
  • Printing

CorelDraw X7 Course Outline:

  • Introduction & the basics of CorelDraw X7
  • Corel Draw X7 Tools
  • Creating Basic Logos in Corel Draw X7
  • Understanding Corel Draw X7 Menus
  • Creating Advance Projects in Corel Draw X7

Adobe Photoshop Course Outline:

  • Evaluating and outputting high quality artwork for print, web and mobile devices.
  • Digital image characteristics, and how they are constructed.
  • Exploring and navigating the application interface, and tailor its appearance to suit your needs.
  • Managing and saving files to the appropriate size and format by exploring different output scenarios.
  • Working with layers to maximise your workflow efficiency.
  • Selecting regions of artwork to apply local edits.
  • Blending artwork and creating montages.
  • Applying filters and effects to add creative flair and finesse to your artwork.
  • Creating simple vector objects.
  • Removing blemishes and defects in photographs
  • Correcting colour and exposure in artwork.
